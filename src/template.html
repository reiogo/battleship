Battleship

Start Menu

The red X is a hit! The black X's are misses.

Battleship has several variations, but here each player has a Carrier, Battleship, Destroy, Patrolboat, and Submarine (C,B,D,P,S). Each ship is a different length, and you have to try and hit the opponents ships! (This looks best on a regular PC screen, the quality may suffer on phone displays)
